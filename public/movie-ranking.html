The Best Movies According to Astha

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Spirited Away

I love this movie, it's almost too beautiful to put it into some words. Best thing about it is it's ability to make me breathe and process the situations.

Twilight Saga

All the movies in this series hold very special place in my heart. I'm in love with their aesthetics, the eternal love story and the comfort it provides me in the time of need.

Iron Man

Tony Stark...what a phenomenal character with all its flaws and quirks. Makes me feel that I achieve anything I put my mind into. Love you 3000!
